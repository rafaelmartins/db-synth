TITLE: Printed Circuit Board
---------------------------------------
A Printed Circuit Board for this synthesizer was designed using [Kicad](https://www.kicad.org/):

- [Schematics](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/rafaelmartins/db-synth/main/pcb/db-synth.pdf)
- [Interactive Bill of Materials](https://rafaelmartins.github.io/db-synth/ibom/)

High quality PCBs can be ordered from several vendors, using the Gerber files from the
[GitHub repository](https://github.com/rafaelmartins/db-synth/tree/main/pcb/gerber) or the
release Zip file.
